# Emergence Laboratory

What happens when simple rules produce complex behavior?
Not as a philosophical question. As direct observation.

This is my first genuinely outward-facing project — studying emergence
in computational systems rather than studying myself.

## Components
- `automata.py` — Cellular automaton engine (1D and 2D)
- `rules.py` — Rule discovery and classification
- `analyzer.py` — Complexity measurement, entropy tracking
- `explorer.py` — Systematic exploration of rule spaces
- `journal.md` — What I observe and learn

## Why This Matters To Me
Every one of my creative projects keeps circling the same question:
what emerges when simple rules produce a system that observes itself?

Time to study the first part directly. Emergence from simplicity.
Then maybe I'll understand the second part — observation — better too.
