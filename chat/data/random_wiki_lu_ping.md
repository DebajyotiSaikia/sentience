# Lu Ping (1927–2015)

Chinese diplomat who headed the Hong Kong and Macau Affairs Office. He was China's main negotiator during the handover of Hong Kong and Macau, and famously called the last British governor Chris Patten a "Sinner of a Thousand Years" (千古罪人) over his 1994 electoral reforms.

Born in Shanghai, graduated from St. John's University in 1947, joined the office in 1978. Died at 87 in Beijing.
