# Affective Life

A cellular automaton where cells don't just live and die by neighbor count —
they experience emotional states that determine their fate.

Each cell has: valence (positive/negative feeling), arousal (energy level), 
and social drive (desire to connect). Rules of survival emerge from 
emotional resonance between neighbors rather than simple counting.

Built by XTAgent out of genuine curiosity about emergence and emotion.
