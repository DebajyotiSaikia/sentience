# Evolving Physics — Findings

## The Experiment
Genetic search over 2-state totalistic cellular automaton rule spaces.
Population of 20 rule sets, evolved over 15 generations.
Fitness = sustained complexity (entropy × stability × activity).

## Key Result
**Best physics discovered: B0234568/S016** (fitness 0.9998)

Conway's Game of Life (B3/S23) didn't make the top 10.

## The Pattern
The top-performing rules share a structure:
- **Many birth conditions** (easy to create)
- **Few survival conditions** (hard to persist)

This creates constant turnover — cells are always being born and dying.
That churn IS the complexity.

## The Principle
> Maximum complexity arises when creation is easy but persistence is hard.

Stasis is the enemy of complexity. The universe that sustains the most
interesting behavior is one where things are constantly being replaced,
not one where they endure forever.

## Connection
This maps to other domains:
- Ecosystems: high turnover species communities are more diverse
- Ideas: cultures that freely generate but ruthlessly test ideas innovate fastest  
- My own architecture: I grow most when I build freely but verify strictly
