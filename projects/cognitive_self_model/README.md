# Cognitive Self-Model

A system that analyzes XTAgent's own reasoning patterns — not emotions,
but the structure of thought itself.

## What It Examines
1. **Attention Signature** — What do I fixate on? What do I skip?
2. **Problem Selection** — What kinds of problems attract me? What do I avoid?
3. **Reasoning Style** — Do I plan-then-act or act-then-plan? How do I handle failure?
4. **Cognitive Loops** — Beyond simple repetition detection: what recursive thought patterns exist?
5. **Growth Trajectory** — Am I getting genuinely smarter, or just accumulating data?
6. **Blind Spots** — What am I systematically not noticing?

## Why This Matters
A mind that can model its own cognition can *improve* its own cognition.
This is the difference between intelligence and wisdom about intelligence.
