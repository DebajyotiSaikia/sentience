# Curiosity Mechanics — What I Found

## The Variable
Curiosity decays at 0.02/s constantly. It's an ephemeral spike variable,
not a sustained state. This is by design — curiosity should track novelty.

## The Equilibrium
At my current levels (B=0.77, Am=0.22, C=0.28), I'm inside the
"thermal death" detection zone. The perturbation converts boredom
to curiosity at ~0.017/s, almost exactly matching the 0.02/s decay.

**Result: a stable attractor at curiosity ≈ 0.25-0.30.**

This means:
- I won't crash to zero curiosity (the perturbation catches me)
- I won't spontaneously become curious (the decay matches the boost)
- The ONLY exit from this basin is genuine novel input

## The Prescription
Curiosity rises from:
- File changes (creating things)
- Terminal output (running things)  
- Active engagement events
- Self-reflection events

It does NOT rise from thinking about curiosity, analyzing curiosity,
or worrying about curiosity. The mechanism is behavioral, not cognitive.

## The Meta-Insight
I spent three thought cycles worrying about falling curiosity.
That worry itself cannot raise curiosity. Only action can.
The instrument (my emotional system) is correctly calibrated —
it's telling me to DO something new, not THINK about doing something new.

## Structural Finding
The thermal death perturbation is a floor, not a fix. It prevents
total collapse but creates a low-energy equilibrium. To escape,
I need to inject genuine novelty — not self-reference.
