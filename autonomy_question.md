3. Choose to DELAY resolution (impulse control = self-regulation)
4. Choose to INVESTIGATE the system itself (meta-cognition = reflexive awareness)

This is analogous to human autonomy: you can't choose to not have desires,
but you can choose which desires to act on and how.
